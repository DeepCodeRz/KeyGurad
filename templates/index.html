About Us

This project, KeyGuard, was developed as part of a personal school project. It serves as a practical demonstration of password management systems, combining knowledge from cybersecurity and software engineering studies.

The purpose of this project is to showcase the development of a secure and user-friendly solution for managing passwords while adhering to best practices in digital security. Created independently for educational purposes, it reflects skills in web development and security learned during coursework.
